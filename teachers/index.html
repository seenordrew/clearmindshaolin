Andrew first started practicing chi kung in 2005. He enjoys countless benefits from his chi kung practice, just some of those being: daily peace and joy, clear thinking, overall improved learning, excellent health (including during flu season), little to no jetlag while traveling internationally and smiling from the heart.

Andrew is an authorized teacher of Shaolin Cosmos Chi Kung as part of the Shaolin Wahnam Institute. He has learned from Sifu Wong in Ecuador, Costa Rica, the United States, Canada and Malaysia. He also organized courses for Sifu Wong in the Galapagos Islands and San Francisco, California. He is currently focusing on sharing the Shaolin arts in Houston, Texas where he has begun teaching chi kung.

Andrew Roberts

Grandmaster Wong, popularly known as Sifu Wong, is a world renowned teacher of the Shaolin Arts, namely Kung Fu, Chi Kung and Zen. Sifu Wong has been teaching for over 30 years and he regularly travels around the world spreading these precious arts to help people restore good health, vitality and longevity in their lives.

In 1997 he was recognized as "Chi Kung Master of the Year" at the World Chi Kung Congress in San Francisco. He has written numerous books on the Shaolin arts, including The Art of Chi Kung, The Complete Book of Tai Chi Chuan, The Art of Shaolin Kung Fu and The Complete Book of Chinese Medicine.
