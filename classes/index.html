Location

Andrew is currently available to teach chi kung in Houston, TX.

Course Structure

A chi kung course lasts eight weeks, meeting once a week. The focus is on learning the fundamental skills required for practicing genuine chi kung, such as: being relaxed, entering a chi kung state of mind and generating an energy flow. Every week a new chi kung pattern is learned and students gradually learn how to practice chi kung on their own. At the end of the course, students will be ready to continue with their independent daily practice, which blossoms over time.
