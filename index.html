Clear Mind Shaolin

Clear Mind Shaolin is one branch of the Shaolin Wahnam Institute, which is a school of genuine, traditional Shaolin arts founded by Grandmaster Wong Kiew Kit with the aim of preserving the precious arts for current and future generations. Founded in 1982 in Malaysia, the school has grown internationally, with certified teachers in over 30 countries. Shaolin Wahnam focuses on teaching Chi Kung, Shaolin Kung Fu, Taijiquan (Tai Chi Chuan) and Zen. These arts have helped thousands of people reclaim good health, vitality and a joyful life.

As Grandmaster Wong says, “To be healthy is natural; to be unhealthy is unnatural.” Our natural state is to be healthy and happy, but it’s become widely accepted that it’s normal to be stressed, unhealthy and even unhappy. Practicing the Shaolin arts is an excellent way to return to our natural state of joy and good health.

Extensive information about the Shaolin arts can be found on Grandmaster Wong’s homepage: shaolin.org.
