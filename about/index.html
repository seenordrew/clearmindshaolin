Who We Are

In modern society it's often accepted that it's normal to be unhealthy, stressed and unhappy. While it may be common, it is actually abnormal to live in such a state. As Grandmaster Wong Kiew Kit says, "To be healthy is natural; to be unhealthy is unnatural." The goal of Clear Mind Shaolin is to help people return to their natural state of good health and inner joy.

Practicing the Shaolin Arts is a truly fantastic way to regain good health, vitality and joyful living. The process itself is quite simple; by practicing an art like Shaolin Cosmos Chi Kung you flush away all the physical, energetic and mental blockages that are inhibiting your natural state of well-being. Well-being is already present - it's your natural state - but it has been covered up by many layers of rubbish. A common example is a lightbulb that is emitting light perfectly well, but the light cannot be seen because the lightbulb has accumulated so much dust.
